Presentation

This is the project 3 of our first year in Gtech, we get to create a website about a video game in one page.
We choose Final Fantasy XV for this project because we love the Final Fantasy's series (because ff VII LMAO) and we thought (more Karma than Clément) it's the most beautiful game we have ever seen, so it's the perfect game for creating a really beautiful website.
On the website, you can consult a part of the wiki of the series Final Fantasy and Final Fantasy XV.
We thought it's important for the website's atmosphere to put the FFXV's theme song in it.
You can view (or review for the best of us) the beautiful Omen's trailer for Final Fantasy XV and some Gameplay.There is his score on Metacritic, News from Square Enix's official website, you have links to buy the game and a (too) big gallery with pictures of the game.

Realisation

For the beginnings, we were a bit long at the start but once the bases assimilated We moved on quickly. We did not really make a mock-up of the site but we concentrated more on the distribution of tasks on our Trello, which helped us to do things more effectively. As we really wanted to focus on the aesthetics of the site, we spent a lot of time looking for fonts or background images that would all stick to the aesthetics of Final Fantasy games.

Concretely the most complicated for us was to do something estethic. It’s been a real big job finding good quality images in JPG and finding good ones PNG images, and after finding them, all processed to optimize the best possible. Then the most complex was the background image which in the first place was too aggressive for the eyes, so we had to change it without giving it too much time either but we succeeded in the end came out a pretty beautiful background that respects what we wanted at the base. The processing of the images was also among the most complex spots, we had to reduce them without reducing the quality we really wanted to keep.

The effort was also put on the atmosphere and the multiple Easter Egg of the site. Indeed, it was not simple but the addition of a background music was quite complex vis à vis the idea that we originally had. We wanted to have a music that starts automatically when loading the site but this to Finally was abandoned because too complex for us to realize at that time. We are also amused to put some hidden buttons in the site. Hoping that you will manage to find them all (it is quite simple in real life).

Result

The goal of this project for us was to provide a site as complete as possible and of which we are proud, and it’s rather successful! (At least I hope for the full side) Despite the stress of defense, we are rather proud to present this site and it was a great adventure to discover HTML to create this site. This project has allowed us to improve in the organization of work and we have well introduces the HTML and CSS language. The only thing that leaves us a little on our hunger is that we would have liked to make a site again more advanced and aesthetically more beautiful.
